## 1. introduction to Hoczi
**Study Guide for Students and Teachers :**
- Users can take quizzes with or without logging in or registering an account.
- Logged-in users can review their results and retry quizzes.
- Supports a multi-tenant model, where one tenant can have multiple teachers and students.
- Tenant owners can add users as tenant members.
- teacher can create classes, can add members to class as student or other teachers
- teacher can create assignments, lessons, quiz, sessions, can assign assignments to students
- assignments can be lessons, text, file or quizzes
- after assign to a student member, it will appear in students side, student can do quiz, can do assignment and mark as complete
- student can comment on assignment, teacher either
- by creating questions then create quiz, then quiz_sessions, teacher can give the due at, during times also
- after students finshing, the results will be appear at teacher side
- teacher can also make as complete then user cannot do the quiz, teacher also cannot create new session
**Other parts of platform:**
- Games: with mini games user can play as entertament with joy and fun
- Students and Teachers can also have schedules so they can arrange time slot for every day and week
